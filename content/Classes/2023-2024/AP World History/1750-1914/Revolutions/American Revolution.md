# Causes
- Taxes
- Harsh British rule

- Independence
- Same rights as Britain
- Democracy
- "No taxation without representation"
# Opponents
- Britain
- France actually helped
# Results
- Set up new government with **Declaration of Independence** in 1776, later creating constitution
- Constitutional Democracy
- United States
