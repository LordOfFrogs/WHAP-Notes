Used when [[Exponential Response Formula|ERF]] fails because r is a root of the characteristic polynomial
$$
x_{p}=\frac{ate^{rt}}{p'(r)}
$$
If that fails,
$$
x_{p}(t)=\frac{at^{2}e^{rt}}{p''(r)}
$$
and so on
$$
x_{p}(t)=\frac{at^{k}e^{rt}}{p^{(k)}(r)}
$$
