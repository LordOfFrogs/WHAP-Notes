$$
\text{rank}(\mathbf{A})+\text{nullity}(\mathbf{A})=\dim(\text{domain}(\mathbf{A}))
$$
The [[Rank]] plus the [[Nullity]] of a [[matrix]] is the [[Dimension]] of its domain
Also stated as
$$
\dim(\text{im}(\mathbf{A}))+\dim(\ker(\mathbf{A}))=n=\dim(\text{domain}(\mathbf{A}))
$$
